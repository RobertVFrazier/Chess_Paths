Information

Welcome to Chess Paths, a simple puzzle game.

In the title screen, just click on the queen to start the puzzle. This will take you to the main game board. The buttons there will give you everything you need to play the game.

Credits:

This puzzle was created over a century ago by Henry Ernest Dudeney.


The Chess Paths logo and all of the buttons were created using Xara 3D Maker 7, modified with Photoshop CC.


The photograph of the chess queen is from a chess set you can buy online here.


The queen symbol used in the game came from here, modified with Photoshop CC.
