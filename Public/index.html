Information

Welcome to Chess Paths, a simple puzzle game.


In the title screen, just click on the queen to start the puzzle. This will take you to the main game board. The buttons there will give you everything you need to play the game.

Rules

Instructions for playing the Chess Paths queen puzzle.


Click or tap on the chessboard to place the queen. (Keyboard users: tab to the chessboard, use the arrow keys to move, then hit Space or Enter to place the queen.) Placing the queen does not count as a move, but it does mark the square as visited.


You have fourteen moves to visit each square on the chessboard and end on a square next to the square you started from. A square is visited if the queen has either moved through it, or landed on it.


So, if you start on square A1, lower left corner, and move to square H1, lower right corner, that is one move, with eight squares visited.


Only moves which are legal for a chess queen to make will be allowed. If you are not familiar with the legal moves for a queen, click the Moves button below. Short version: a queen can move in straight lines only: horizontally, vertically, or in 45 degree diagonals, any number of squares, until she reaches the edge of the board.



Use the Undo and Redo buttons (the curving left and right arrow buttons) as often as you like. There is no penalty in the game for undoing moves. The Reset button will reset the game to before you placed the queen on the board.


Click the Load/Save button to open a new screen. You will need to sign up (or log in, if you have already signed up) to save or load games. You will only need to give a user name and password. It's only to keep different people's games separate. I won't collect any other information from you.


That's it. Have fun, and good luck!
